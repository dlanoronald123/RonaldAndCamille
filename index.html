How we met

We first met on May 14, 2019, as workmates, exchanging casual, work-related conversations. Camille would always greet Ronald with a smile and a "Good morning, Nald," to which Ronald would sometimes reply depending on his mood. Unbeknownst to each other, both had secret crushes.

Ronald, focused on his studies, chose not to act on his feelings. After more than two years as colleagues, Ronald left the company due to relocation, and Camille followed three months later.

After two months of no contact, Ronald messaged Camille, and their conversations soon grew frequent. Ronald began courting her, often picking her up from work and going on dates. Months later, Camille said yes, and within a month, Ronald promised he would marry her.
