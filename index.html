How we met

We met on May 14, 2019, as workmates. As colleagues, we only had casual conversations, always related to work.

Every morning, whenever we ran into each other, Camille would always greet Ronald with a "Good morning, Nald," accompanied by a smile. Sometimes, Ronald would greet back, but other times he wouldn’t, depending on his mood. Camille didn’t know that Ronald had actually had a crush on her since 2019, and Camille felt the same way about him. Since he was studying online at the time, he decided to focus on that until he finished.

For more than two years as workmates, Ronald decided to end his contract due to the company’s relocation, while Camille stayed for three more months before she finally decided to leave as well.

After two months without any communication, Ronald finally decided to message Camille. We caught up with each other until our conversations became more frequent, and that’s when Ronald decided to court her. He would always pick Camille up from work, and they would go on dates afterward.

Months later, Camille decided to say yes to Ronald. Just one month into their relationship, Ronald promised that he would marry her.
